Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI help photographers book more clients?

Speed is everything. Couples inquiring about wedding photography often contact 5-10 photographers at once. AI responds to every inquiry instantly with your pricing, availability, and a booking link — making you the first photographer they talk to.

Can AI handle photography contracts and invoices?

Yes. AI workflows can automatically send contracts (via tools like HoneyBook or Dubsado), collect signatures, generate invoices, and send payment reminders — all triggered by the booking event.

Will AI feel impersonal for my photography brand?

AI handles the admin so you can be MORE personal where it matters — during consultations, shoots, and gallery reveals. Clients don't want a personal touch on invoice reminders; they want it during the shoot.
