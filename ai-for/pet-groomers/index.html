Frequently Asked Questions

Can AI handle pet grooming appointments?

Yes. AI booking systems let pet parents select breed, service type, and groomer preference — then book 24/7. It handles special instructions (sensitive skin, anxiety, etc.) and syncs with your calendar.

How does AI remind pet parents to rebook?

AI tracks each pet's last visit and grooming interval (every 4 weeks for doodles, 6 weeks for labs, etc.) and sends a personalized text with a one-tap booking link when it's time.

Is this affordable for a solo pet groomer?

Basic AI booking and reminder systems cost $50-$100/month. Preventing just one no-show per week covers that cost many times over.
