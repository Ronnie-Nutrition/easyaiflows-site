Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI help fitness studios retain members?

AI monitors member check-in data and identifies patterns — like someone who usually comes 3x/week but hasn't visited in 10 days. It automatically sends personalized re-engagement messages before the member decides to cancel.

Can AI book classes and trial sessions?

Yes. AI chatbots on your website, Instagram, and Facebook can show class schedules, answer questions about pricing, and book trial sessions — all without staff involvement.

Does this work for boutique studios or just big gyms?

AI automation is especially powerful for boutique studios where every member matters. The personal touch of automated follow-ups actually feels more boutique than big-gym mass emails.
