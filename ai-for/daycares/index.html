Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI help daycares with enrollment?

AI responds to every enrollment inquiry instantly — by phone, text, email, or web form. It shares availability, pricing, and program details, then books a tour. Parents are more likely to enroll when they get fast, helpful responses.

Can AI manage a daycare waitlist?

Yes. AI maintains a prioritized waitlist and automatically contacts families when spots open. It tracks responses, moves to the next family if there's no reply, and keeps everyone informed of their position.

Is AI appropriate for childcare communication?

AI handles administrative communication (billing, scheduling, closures) while your staff focuses on the personal, care-related updates. Parents appreciate timely admin messages, and your staff has more time for the kids.
