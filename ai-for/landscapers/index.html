Frequently Asked Questions

Can AI provide landscaping estimates?

AI can collect property details (address, square footage, service needed) and provide instant ballpark estimates. For complex jobs, it schedules an on-site visit. This qualifies leads before you drive out.

How does AI help with landscaping crew scheduling?

AI optimizes daily crew schedules by geographic zone, reducing drive time between jobs. It handles recurring service schedules, weather-related reschedules, and crew assignments automatically.

Does AI marketing work for seasonal landscaping services?

Extremely well. AI sends targeted campaigns to your customer list at the right time — spring cleanup reminders in February, aeration offers in September, holiday lighting in October. Past customers convert at 5-10x the rate of cold leads.
