Frequently Asked Questions

Can AI handle barbershop appointment booking?

Yes. AI booking systems let clients choose their barber, pick a service, and select a time — all online, 24/7. It syncs with your calendar so there are never double-bookings.

How does AI get clients to rebook?

AI tracks when each client last visited and sends a personalized text at the right interval (e.g., 3 weeks for fades, 5 weeks for longer styles) with a one-tap booking link.

Is this too expensive for a small barbershop?

Basic AI booking and reminder systems start at $50-$100/month — less than one missed appointment costs you. It pays for itself on day one.
