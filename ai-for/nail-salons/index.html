Frequently Asked Questions

Can AI book nail salon appointments?

Yes. AI booking handles service selection, tech preference, time selection, and even deposits — all without a phone call. Clients book from Instagram DMs, your website, or Google.

How does AI help with nail salon no-shows?

AI sends reminders at 48hr and 2hr marks, requires deposits for high-value services, and instantly fills cancellations from your waitlist. Most salons see no-shows drop 50-70%.

Can AI run my nail salon's social media?

AI can schedule posts, respond to DMs with booking links, and even generate caption ideas for your nail art photos. You still take the photos — AI handles the rest.
